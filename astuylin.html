Stuyvesant High School offers one of the country's most innovative and exciting computer science programs.

Courses in the sequence start with the assumption that students have never been exposed to computer science. Students completing the sequence will be exposed to advanced college level subjects.

The program emphasizes a variety of problem solving techniques and exposes students to new and different ways of thinking. There is also an emphasis on the relationships between computer science and other subject areas.
